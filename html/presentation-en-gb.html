More about Glup

Principes

Glup is a workshop to help you turn yout text-based exercises into mini-serious games

Glup is based on the dissociation between the educational apparatus and the gameplay.

Selecting an educational goal.

An educational gooal lies in such instructions as "underline the words which...." or "find the right word order for this sentence".

Selecting a gameplay

For each educational goal Glup offers gameplays with interactivity adapted to the structure of the exercise.

Customizing games

Each game an be customizez (size, difficulty level, instructions, etc.)

Downloading and publishing games

When happy with the results you can dowlnoad the game as a flash file you can publish on a website, a blog or homework on-line notebook, e-mail to your students via the school network our a USB key.

Recycling corpus

You can copy-paste into Glup texts of exercises that you created for your classes.

Advantages of Glup

Games are prone to create in people a state called "flow" by psychologist Csikszentmihalyi.

Thus with Glup you can make for part of the deficit of attention of some pupils by adding a layer of interactivity to your exercises.

How Glup works

Technically, Glup is 3-tier web software with on-the-fly flash compilation possibilities

Evolutions of Glup

This version is but a limited-contents prototype (only one educational goal and two games).

Our aim is to enrich Culp thanks to the addition of:

  • new educational goals
  • new gameplays
  • more languages

These evolutions will only depend on Glup's popularity and users' contributions.
